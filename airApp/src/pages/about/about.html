About How safe is the air we're breathing? This isn't as easy of a question to answer as it may seem. How would we know? It looks clear, usually smells fine, but does that mean it's safe? To answer this question, we're building a series of sensors in the neighborhood around Grace In Action to figure out for ourselves, and then to let the world know. You can use this information to protect yourself, advocate for change, and to breathe safe!
